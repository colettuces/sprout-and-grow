About Sprout & Grow

Sprout & Grow is dedicated to inspiring a lifelong love of gardening. We provide high-quality plant kits, seeds, and expert advice to gardeners of all skill levels. Our mission is to make gardening accessible, joyful, and sustainable for everyone.

Founded with a passion for nature, Sprout & Grow believes that even the smallest seed can spark a big change. Whether you’re starting your first windowsill garden or expanding a backyard oasis, we’re here to help you flourish.
